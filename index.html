A Little About Us

Il Mediterraneo is a restaurant that takes cooking into a whole new realm. Its non-traditional way of cuisine will leave you in awe with every bite you take.

Our vibe is that of Mediterranean countries with a strong Italian influence. With our carefully selected ingredients, most of which are imported from Italy are sure to take your taste buds on a journey to the moon and back. We are proud of being able to bring a little piece of culinary magic into your world.
